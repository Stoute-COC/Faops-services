Frequently Asked Questions

We take all necessary precautions to ensure your account's safety. Our boosters use VPNs and play carefully to avoid detection. While no service can guarantee 100% safety, we've never had a client banned for using our services.

Completion time depends on the rank and division you're boosting to. Most boosts are completed within 1-3 days. For higher ranks (GC and above), it may take up to a week depending on queue times and your current MMR.

For ranks below GC1, you can choose to play with the booster (requires +10$) without sharing your account. For GC1 and above, we'll need your account access as these ranks require more consistent play.

We accept PayPal, cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum), and gift cards (Amazon, Steam). Payment is required upfront before we start your boost.

We offer partial refunds if we're unable to complete the boost as promised. However, we've never had to issue a refund as we always deliver what we promise. If you have any concerns during the boost, contact us immediately.
